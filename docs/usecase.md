# Use Cases

PulSeed's narrative use cases are future scenarios. They are helpful for
understanding the product direction, but they are not turnkey workflows in the
current package.

Read:

- [Future Use Cases](roadmap/use-cases.md) for roadmap scenarios
- [Status](status.md) for what exists today
- [Guide](guide.md) for current workflows you can try now

Roadmap examples are not medical, veterinary, financial, legal, or business
advice. Use [Runtime](runtime.md) and [Configuration](configuration.md) for
current behavior and setup details.
